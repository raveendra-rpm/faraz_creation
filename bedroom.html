Styling Your Bedroom

The bedroom is your ultimate retreat, and the foundation of its comfort lies in the rug you choose. Placing a large, plush rug partially under your bed ensures you step onto a soft, warm surface every morning.

Opt for soothing colors like soft blues, muted creams, or gentle earth tones found in our Oushak and Gabbeh collections to promote relaxation and restful sleep.

  • ✓ Sizes: 8x10 or 9x12 recommended for King/Queen beds.
  • ✓ Material: Soft wool or silk blends for maximum comfort.
  • ✓ Placement: Leave 18-24 inches of rug exposed around the sides and foot of the bed.
