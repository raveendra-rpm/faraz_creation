Styling Dark Wood Floors

Dark hardwoods provide a luxurious base but can make a room feel heavy if not balanced correctly. Lighter rugs—creams, soft blues, and muted grays—reflect light and define zones within your room beautifully.

Alternatively, you can embrace an opulent aesthetic by using rich jewel tones like ruby reds and emerald greens, which pull out the warm undertones of espresso or mahogany flooring.

  • ✓ Brighten: Use light-colored Oushak or Tabriz rugs to lift the room's energy.
  • ✓ Dramatize: Use Heriz or Shiraz rugs for an atmospheric, vintage study feel.
  • ✓ Texture: High-pile wool adds a cozy contrast to sleek, hard wood.
