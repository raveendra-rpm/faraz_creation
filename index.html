Our Heritage

- Craftsmanship, Culture, Tradition -

At Faraz Carpets, we take pride in a legacy built on craftsmanship, culture, and tradition... Since the early 1990s, we have been dedicated to producing exquisite Indian handmade carpets & rugs that blend tradition with innovation... Every piece we create is a testament to the art of a passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

With an unwavering focus on quality, design, and authenticity, Faraz Carpets brings timeless beauty into contemporary spaces. Our collections reflect a deep respect for historical rug-making techniques, honoring their intricate details... Whether adorning a classic home or commercial space, each Faraz Carpets rug is more than decor — it's a story woven in thread.
