About Faraz

Our Story

At Faraz Handcrafted Carpets, we believe that a carpet is more than just a furnishing—it's a legacy of culture, craftsmanship, and creativity. Founded in the early 1990s, Faraz has grown from a modest manufacturing unit into a respected name in the world of handmade rugs and carpets, both in India and globally.

With over three decades of experience, we continue to honour the age-old traditions of rug weaving while embracing the innovation and aesthetics of contemporary design. Our roots lie deep in India's rich textile heritage. Every rug we create is a result of meticulous craftsmanship, passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

From the spinning of natural fibres to the intricate knotting and finishing process, our carpets are entirely handmade—reflecting the soul and skill of the hands that weave them.

Today, Faraz carpets adorn homes, hotels, galleries, and institutions around the world, celebrated for their quality, uniqueness, and artistry. At our core, we remain a family-driven enterprise—rooted in values of integrity, excellence, and a genuine love for handmade rugs.

At Faraz Handcrafted Carpets, every rug tells a story. Let us help you find one that becomes part of yours.
