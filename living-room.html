Styling Your Living Room

The living room rug defines the conversation area. A generously sized rug makes the room feel larger and more cohesive. It should ideally be large enough to slide under the front legs of all major pieces of furniture.

Consider the traffic level—for high-traffic living spaces, tightly woven wools like those in our Isfahan or Shiraz collections provide durability while maintaining extraordinary beauty over decades.

  • ✓ Sizes: 8x10, 9x12, or even 10x14 depending on the space.
  • ✓ Material: Wool blends or pure wool for resilience and softness.
  • ✓ Placement: All front legs of sofas and chairs should rest on the rug.
