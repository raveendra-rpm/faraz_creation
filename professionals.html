Hello Designers,

Partner with
Faraz Creation

At Faraz Creation, we partner with interior designers to bring their visions to life through custom-made, premium handmade rugs and carpets. Whether you're designing for a private residence, commercial space, or luxury project, we offer personalized solutions tailored to your specific needs.

With over five generations of expertise in hand-knotting and weaving, we combine artisanal heritage with modern design sensibilities to deliver rugs that are truly one-of-a-kind.

From initial consultation to final delivery, we make every step seamless.

01

Initial Consultation

Share your vision, palette, and requirements with our team for a personalized experience.

02

Sample Approval

Seamless Delivery
